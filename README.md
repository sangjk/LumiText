## 🎯 About

LumiText is a lightweight yet powerful **macOS tool** that lets you capture text from anywhere on your screen — images, PDFs, videos, websites, even paused video frames — and instantly recognize, translate, and copy it. No more manually retyping. Everything happens locally on your Mac.

### ✨ Key Features

- **📸 Screenshot Text Recognition** — press a shortcut, select any area of your screen, and the text is instantly recognized and copied to your clipboard. Supports both full-screen capture and precise region selection.
- **🌍 Multi-language OCR** — recognizes Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic, and more. Automatic language detection — no need to switch settings manually.
- **🔄 Instant Translation** — translate recognized text between multiple languages with one click. Toggle between original and translated text seamlessly.
- **📱 QR Code & Barcode Scanning** — scan QR codes, Aztec, DataMatrix, PDF417, Code128, Code39, EAN13, EAN8 and more, directly from your screen.

## 🏆 Why LumiText?

**⚡ Speed & Performance**
Built to run natively on macOS. Fully leverages Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) for millisecond-level text recognition. No cloud latency — everything processes locally on your device.

**🔒 Privacy First**
All OCR processing happens on your Mac. Your screenshots and recognized text never leave your device. Core functions work completely offline — no internet connection required.

**🎯 Simple & Non-intrusive**
Lives quietly in your menu bar. Trigger it with a global hotkey from any app. Automatically copies results to clipboard. No complex settings, no learning curve.

**🌐 30 Interface Languages**
Full internationalization support across 30 languages, including Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Arabic, and many more. The app adapts to your system language automatically.

## 🚀 How It Works

**① Press ⌘ ⇧ S** anywhere — from your browser, PDF reader, video player, or any app.

**② Select the area** — drag your mouse to select the text region. Full screen or precise crop both supported.

**③ Paste anywhere** — recognized text is automatically copied. Press ⌘V to paste into any application.

## 🎯 Quick Start Guide

1. **📲 Download & Install** — Get LumiText from the Mac App Store
2. **🔐 Grant Permissions** — On first launch, grant "Screen Recording" and "Accessibility" permissions in System Preferences
3. **⌨️ Start Using** — Press `⌘⇧S` anytime to activate LumiText from any application

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Function |
|----------|----------|
| `⌘⇧S` | Screenshot OCR Recognition |
| `⌘⇧V` | Clipboard History |
| `⌘⇧T` | Toggle Translation |
