# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability in LumiText, please report it responsibly.

**Do not open a public issue.** Instead, email us at:

📧 **sangkl@163.com**

Please include:

- A detailed description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Your macOS version and device information

We will respond within 5 business days and keep you updated on the resolution progress.

## Supported Versions

| Version | Supported |
|---------|-----------|
| 2.0.x   | ✅ Active |
| 1.0.x   | ✅ Security fixes |
| < 1.0.0 | ❌ End of life |

## Our Commitment

- All OCR processing is performed locally on-device — no data leaves your Mac.
- We do not collect or store your screenshots or recognized text.
- We follow Apple's security best practices for macOS applications.

## Responsible Disclosure

We appreciate the security community's efforts. If your report leads to a fix, we're happy to acknowledge your contribution (with your permission).
